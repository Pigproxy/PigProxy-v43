How to add hosted games (admin only)
1. Upload your file to /public/games/ on the server.
2. Open public/games.html and uncomment the hosted game card template.
3. Set the href to /games/your-filename.zip and fill in title, size, and description.
4. Restart the server — it shows up immediately.
Keep it appropriate. Only download games you are allowed to have on school devices. Check with your teacher or school IT if you are unsure.
